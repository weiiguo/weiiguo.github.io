About me

I received the B.Eng. in electronic engineering from the Univeristy of Electronic Science and Techonology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China, in 2017, and Ph.D. degree in computer and information engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ), China, in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Chuan Huang. I was a visiting student with the Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data, Shenzhen, and Purple Mountain Laboratories, Nanjing. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow with the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Engineering (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Khaled B. Letaief and Prof. Jun Zhang.
